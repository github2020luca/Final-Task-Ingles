Bariloche is a place well known by the tourists it receives every year, so we are going to recommend which places you could visit:

I have visited The road of the 7 lakes It was beautiful! The road of the 7 lakes The section joins the towns of San Martín de los Andes and Villa La Angostura in the Province of Neuquén and owes its name to the fact that seven lakes can be seen during its journey.

Also in bariloche you can enjoy extreme sports

I have done extreme sports in Bariloche! It was incredible The extreme sports that can be done are: climbing a mountain on foot or by bicycle, canopy, paragliding, kayaking, diving, horseback riding, kitesurfing, skiing, snowboarding and some other activities.
